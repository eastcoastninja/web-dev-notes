Here's a time line of Elon's life:

  • 1971 - Born to engineer father and model mother
  • 1979 - Reads 10 hours a day runs out of books reads entire Encyclopedia Britannica
  • 1983 - Makes a video game Blastar sells it for $500
  • 1989 - Starts college in Ontario
  • 1990-1994 - Transfers to UPenn double majoring in physics and economics. Gets Wharton economics degree (and later physics degree)
  • 1995 - Starts Zip2 codes it himself (sleeps in office first 3 months)
  • 1999 - Sells Zip2 to Compaq then forms X.com which transforms to PayPal in 2000
  • 2002 - Sells PayPal to eBay gets $250 million
  • 2002 - Founds SpaceX9
  • 2004 - Invest in Tesla Motors
  • 2007 - SpaceX wins $1.6 billion contract to bring cargo to ISS(International Space Station)
  • 2012 - Tesla begins deliveries of all-electric Model S

    • Still to Come:

  • 2020 - First Hyperloop train starts running making it possible to go to LA from NYC in under 30 minutes.
  • 2025 - Mars Colonial Transporter launches
  • In Our Lifetime - Tickets to Mars are $500,000 a piece
